I was proudly born and raised in the heart of Brooklyn, NY. My family relocated to Augusta, Georgia and had we not moved there, I would have not even acknowledge my love for art. My true passions are painting and photography. I am a recent graduate in Cinema and Photography with a Still Photography concentration from Ithaca College. I specialize in street, portrait, and event photography and I have a great eye for it as well, but I am good at photographing other things too. Most of my personal projects are inspired by my identity emotional, mentally and physically and I want to portray this to viewers.

In addition to my artsy side, I am a real outdoorsy person. I love backpacking, kayaking, whitewater rafting, rock climbing, and so much more. If I could pick where I wanted to live, I would pick London because I attended a study abroad program there and it completely changed me as a person and changed my outlook on my future. Personally, I believe art can influence the imagination that is needed in society to keep it thriving, so I support all who want to go into the arts for a career.
